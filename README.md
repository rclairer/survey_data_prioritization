This tool facilitates collaborative review of the NOAA NWFSC FRAM FRS survey sampling protocols and data collection targets designed to support stock assessments and research. The data are from the NOAA NWFSC West Coast Groundfish Bottomtrawl Survey (WCGBTS) and NOAA NWFSC Hook and Line Survey (HKLS). The data are filtered to include the species and stock areas currently included (or considered for inclusion) in the PFMC Fishery Managment Plan.

*This is a work-in-process, results may be updated.

Launch the tool directly from this site: https://connect.fisheries.noaa.gov/survey-sampling-protocol-review/
